About Goodies

We empower creators and communities with elegant, transparent crowdfunding solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Goodies was built to transform how communities fund the ideas and initiatives they care about. We believe that transparency, design, and trust should define the crowdfunding experience — empowering creators to tell their stories beautifully and helping supporters see their impact clearly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge ambition with opportunity — helping visionaries, small businesses, and communities bring their ideas to life. We exist to make giving and building seamless, meaningful, and authentic.

Authenticity

Every campaign is real, human, and transparent — built on integrity and purpose.

Innovation

We blend design and technology to create a modern crowdfunding experience that feels effortless.

Community

We believe in collective progress — empowering creators and backers to grow together.
